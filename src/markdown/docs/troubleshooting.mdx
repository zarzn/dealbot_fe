# Troubleshooting Guide

Encountering an issue with RebatOn? This guide covers common problems and their solutions to get you back to deal hunting quickly.

## 

### 

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Incorrect email or password**
   - Double-check for typos in your email address
   - Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link
   - Check if caps lock is enabled

2. **Account not verified**
   - Look for a verification email in your inbox (and spam folder)
   - Request a new verification email from the login page

3. **Social login issues**
   - Try logging in directly with your email and password
   - Ensure you're using the same social account you signed up with
   - Check if your social account is connected in Account Settings

4. **Browser issues**
   - Clear your browser cache and cookies
   - Try using incognito/private mode
   - Update your browser to the latest version

### 

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Email in spam folder**
   - Check your spam, junk, promotions, or updates folders
   - Add support@rebaton.ai to your contacts list

2. **Incorrect email address**
   - Verify you entered the correct email during signup
   - Contact support if you need to update your email

3. **Email server delays**
   - Wait up to 15 minutes for delayed emails
   - Try requesting another email after waiting

4. **Email blocking**
   - Temporarily disable email filters
   - Use a different email address if available

### 

This typically happens after multiple failed login attempts or suspicious activity detection.

**How to resolve:**

1. Wait 30 minutes before trying again
2. Reset your password using the "Forgot Password" link
3. Contact support if you continue to experience issues

## 

### 

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Search terms too specific**
   - Use fewer keywords
   - Remove specific model numbers or unique identifiers
   - Try more general category terms

2. **Filters too restrictive**
   - Reset all filters and try again
   - Gradually add filters back one at a time
   - Expand price ranges and other numerical filters

3. **No matching deals currently available**
   - Set up a Deal Goal to be notified when matching deals appear
   - Try similar products or alternative brands
   - Save your search to check back later

4. **Regional availability issues**
   - Check if your region settings are correct in Account Settings
   - Some deals may be restricted to certain countries

### 

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Search query misinterpreted**
   - Use more specific terminology
   - Try using quotation marks around exact phrases
   - Use advanced search operators (described in the [Searching Deals](/how-to-use/searching-deals) guide)

2. **Profile preferences affecting results**
   - Check your preference settings in your profile
   - Temporarily set preferences to default

3. **Browser caching old results**
   - Clear your browser cache
   - Try the search in incognito/private mode

### 

Remember that Deal Scores are relative to both historical prices and market conditions. If you believe a score is wrong:

1. Check the price history tab to understand the context
2. Look at the breakdown of scoring factors
3. Use the "Report Issue" button on the deal page to notify our team
4. Consider if you have different criteria than our algorithm (specialized needs, etc.)

## 

### 

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Notification settings disabled**
   - Check Account Settings > Notifications
   - Ensure the specific notification types are enabled
   - Verify that your notification methods (email, browser, mobile) are enabled

2. **Browser notification permissions**
   - Check that your browser allows notifications from our site
   - Look for the notification icon in your address bar to manage permissions

3. **Email issues**
   - Check spam folders
   - Verify your email address is correct
   - Add our domain to your safe senders list

4. **Mobile app settings**
   - Ensure app notifications are enabled in your device settings
   - Check that background app refresh is enabled
   - Verify you're signed in to the same account on all devices

### 

This can happen when marketplace data hasn't synchronized properly:

1. Click the "Refresh" button on the deal card
2. Visit the deal page to trigger a manual refresh
3. If the issue persists, report it using the "Report Issue" button

### 

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Account limit reached**
   - Free accounts can track up to 3 deals
   - Check your current plan limits in Account Settings
   - Consider upgrading your plan or purchasing tracking slots

2. **Technical glitch**
   - Try logging out and back in
   - Clear your browser cache and cookies
   - Try using a different browser or device

## 

### 

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Goal criteria too specific or restrictive**
   - Edit your goal to broaden the requirements
   - Increase your maximum price
   - Remove some specific feature requirements

2. **Goal description unclear**
   - Add more details about what you're looking for
   - Use specific product categories and common terms
   - Avoid abbreviations the system might not understand

3. **Goal processing delay**
   - New goals can take up to 24 hours to start generating results
   - Check back later or adjust notification settings

### 

If goal recommendations don't match what you're looking for:

1. Click "Not Relevant" on inappropriate recommendations
2. Use the feedback form to explain why it's not relevant
3. Edit your goal with more specific requirements
4. Check that your goal description isn't ambiguous or contradictory

## 

### 

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Recent token usage**
   - Check your token history in Wallet to see recent usage
   - Look for automated renewals or subscription charges

2. **Token expiration**
   - Check if tokens have expired (free tokens expire after 90 days)
   - Look for expiration notices in your email or notifications

3. **Account sync issue**
   - Log out and log back in
   - Try refreshing the wallet page

If you still believe there's an error, contact support with details about the missing tokens.

### 

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Payment method issues**
   - Check that your payment card is valid and not expired
   - Verify billing address matches what's on file with your bank
   - Try a different payment method

2. **Transaction blocks**
   - Check with your bank if they're blocking the transaction
   - Temporarily disable security features that might block online purchases

3. **Regional restrictions**
   - Confirm that purchases are supported in your region
   - Try using a different payment method supported in your region

## 

### 

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Expired content**
   - Deals may have expired or been removed
   - Shares with expiration dates may have elapsed
   - Check your Shared Deals dashboard for status

2. **Privacy settings**
   - Verify the visibility setting (public vs. private)
   - Ensure recipients are logged in if the share is private

3. **Link format issues**
   - Make sure the entire link was copied and shared
   - Try regenerating the share link

### 

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Popup blockers**
   - Disable popup blockers for our site
   - Right-click and copy the link to share manually instead

2. **Social media login issues**
   - Ensure you're logged into the social media platform
   - Try sharing in a different browser

3. **Platform restrictions**
   - Some platforms may restrict certain links
   - Try another sharing method or platform

## 

### 

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **App needs updating**
   - Check for updates in the App Store or Google Play
   - Install the latest version

2. **Device issues**
   - Restart your device
   - Close other apps running in background
   - Check for system updates

3. **Cache issues**
   - Clear the app cache in your device settings
   - Log out and log back in
   - As a last resort, uninstall and reinstall the app

### 

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Camera permissions**
   - Check that camera permissions are enabled for the app
   - Go to device settings to grant necessary permissions

2. **Poor lighting or focus**
   - Ensure adequate lighting
   - Hold the phone steady and give it time to focus
   - Make sure the barcode is clean and undamaged

3. **Unsupported barcode type**
   - Confirm we support the barcode type you're scanning
   - Try manually searching for the product instead

## 

### 

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Internet connection**
   - Check your internet speed at [speedtest.net](https://www.speedtest.net)
   - Try connecting to a different network
   - Disable VPNs or proxies temporarily

2. **Browser issues**
   - Clear browser cache and cookies
   - Disable extensions, especially ad blockers
   - Try our lightweight mode (toggle in footer)
   - Update your browser to the latest version

3. **Device performance**
   - Close other applications and tabs
   - Restart your device
   - Try a different device if possible

### 

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Browser compatibility**
   - Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
   - Update your current browser to the latest version

2. **JavaScript or CSS issues**
   - Ensure JavaScript is enabled
   - Clear browser cache and reload
   - Disable browser extensions that might interfere

3. **Connectivity problems**
   - Check for stable internet connection
   - Try a different network

## 

### 

**Possible causes and solutions:**

1. **Card issues**
   - Check that your card isn't expired
   - Verify you have sufficient funds
   - Confirm billing address matches your card

2. **Bank restrictions**
   - Contact your bank to authorize the transaction
   - Check for fraud alerts or blocks

3. **Payment processor issues**
   - Try a different payment method
   - Wait a few hours and try again

### 

If you've subscribed but don't see your benefits:

1. Check Account Settings > Subscription to verify status
2. Look for confirmation emails or receipts
3. Try logging out and back in
4. Allow up to 15 minutes for systems to sync after subscribing

## 

If you're still experiencing issues after trying these troubleshooting steps:

1. **Check System Status**
   Visit our [status page](https://status.rebaton.ai) to see if there are any known issues or outages.

2. **Contact Support**
   - Use the Help button in the bottom right corner of any page
   - Email support@rebaton.ai with details of your issue
   - Include screenshots, error messages, and steps to reproduce the problem

3. **Visit Our Community Forum**
   Browse or post on our [community forum](/community) where other users might have experienced and solved similar issues.

When contacting support, please include:
- Your account email
- Device and browser/app version
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- What you expected to happen
- What actually happened
- Any error messages you received
- Screenshots if possible

This helps us resolve your issue much faster!
